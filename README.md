# Author
Sha'Rya Weaver - Data Analyst

# Scenario:
You are continuing in your new role as a data analyst for the growing business EmporiUm, which
describes itself as a “virtual student bookstore.” EmporiUm got its start in 2022 following the merger of a chain of college bookstore franchises with an online retailer that sold books and tech gear to students. Today, EmporiUm continues to do a blend of in-store and online sales.

Your team has been asked by one of the regional directors for EmporiUm to analyze sales performance
over the last four years. You must create a Power BI report that highlights key insights, including trends over time and insights into recent sales highlights, to be shared with the regional sales team (including territory managers and individual store managers). You must also identify top-selling books and their authors, as well as any other insights that could be useful. You will roll out this report at the management team meeting for your region.

# Management Team:
Region - West 
Director - Cassie Chambers
States - Colorado & Online Sales
Store Manager - Ellen Lemon(Colorado) & Robin Gilmore and Ryann Kelly(Online Sales)

# Requirements:
Report must consist of at least two pages, with text headers for each page. You are welcome to
customize the theme and formatting of your report however you choose.

Your report must include at least four visuals:
∗ line chart showing sales trends over the full period covered in the data
∗ column or bar chart showing sales performance by category
∗ pie or donut chart showing relative sales for each state in the region
∗ list of top-selling books, including author names, for your sales region (excluding textbooks)

Consider how this information can be used by the sales team to help them make better business
decisions. You will need to pitch this to the sales team in your in-class presentation.
